Father’s Day

Celebrating you, today and always.

Hi Dad,

First of all, Happy Father’s Day.

I know I’m not really one to express things through words, so let me do my best to show my gratitude in this letter. Ever since I was young, you’ve always been a guiding rock in my life— steady, firm, and quietly supportive, even when I didn’t notice. Your guidance has helped shape me into the person I am today.

Thank you for being the foundation I could always rely on. Everything you do for me— driving me places, teaching and helping me learn to drive, and so many other thing— is truly priceless.

I know practically everyone says this about their dad, but you really are the best father I could’ve ever asked for. I truly thank the Lord that I was born to be your child. I don’t know what I’d do without you.

Thank you Dad, forever and always.

With love,
Maelie Yu (15/6/25)
